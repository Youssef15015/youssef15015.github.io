Virtual Qahwa

This project is well underway, and is intended to bind the full forces of Egyptian life to the inevitable rise of Augmented Reality. It is hosted on a discord channel for American Egyptian Students in an Egyptian Student Association, or any student in general. It is symbolized by the traditional Egyptian concept of social public meeting points that are generally meant to escape daily struggles through relaxing,intelligent conversations and board games. Please email joey070@gmail.com for an invite.

Renaissance of American Physicists (RAP)

This project is meant to be a multi-stage project that starts with creating a club at Iowa State University, followed by fundraising and recruitment to eventually separate into an individual organization led by and for students. The goal is to create resources created by and for students that enable the freedom of teaching and research in a comfortable environment free from the restrictions and impedements faced by the common Physics graduate student due to the lack of support of the American higher education system. The later stages will involve obtainable ranks and certificates that will grant each individual to be a display of how RAP can help us grow. Ames will be chosen as its starting base due to the trustworthiness of its citizens and small unemployment rate. It also hosts a very good district of Education.
